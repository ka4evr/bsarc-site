Welcome to N4GM

Brunswick Shores Amateur Radio Club

Welcome to the digital home of the Brunswick Shores Amateur Radio Club (BSARC). We serve the amateur radio community in southeastern North Carolina, supporting emergency communications (ARES/RACES) and promoting the hobby through community events, testing, and regular nets.

Meeting Schedules

Monthly Club Meetings

Meetings are held on the second Wednesday of each month from 6:00 PM to 8:00 PM. The location alternates monthly between Calabash and Supply. Meetings are also normally broadcasted via ZOOM (links are sent directly to members and guests).

Months Location Address
Feb, Apr, Jun, Aug, Oct, Dec Calabash EMS Building 9031 Beach Drive SW, Calabash, NC 28467
Jan, Mar, May, Jul, Sep, Nov BEMC Headquarters 795 Ocean Hwy, Supply, NC 28462

Weekly Breakfast Meeting

A group of local hams meets every Wednesday morning at 8:15 AM at the Tee Time Cafe in Shallotte (126 Holden Beach Road). Club business is often informally discussed, and all are welcome!
