Amateur Radio Web Directory

A comprehensive collection of essential amateur radio tools, federal databases, equipment modifications, and surrounding regional club portals. Local interest portals and emergency services links are highlighted below.

Technical Tools & Calculators

  • 🧮 Amateur Radio RF Exposure Safety Calculator — Quick power density analysis to verify station compliance.
  • 🔧 Modifications for Ham Rigs (Mods.dk) — Manuals, schematics, and alignment steps for commercial transceivers.
  • 📻 Nostalgia Air Antique Radio Resource — Vintage tube data files and schematic archives for historic gear restorers.
  • ⏰ NIST WWV Time Signal Station Portal — Official station operational guidelines and transmission details.

National & State Training Organizations

  • 🏛️ ARRL Main Portal — National association for amateur radio in the US.
  • 🗺️ ARRL North Carolina Section — Local section news, event tracking, and field appointments.
  • 🚨 North Carolina ARES Hub — State emergency network coordination, plans, and training matrices.
  • 🌪️ VoIP SKYWARN / Hurricane Net — Real-time severe weather tactical reporting infrastructure.
  • 💻 FEMA Independent Study Directory — Free online NIMS/ICS core training coursework.

Operating Platforms & Networks

  • 🗺️ EchoLink Portal — Global internet-linking software engine for licensed operators.
  • 🌐 Internet Radio Linking Project (IRLP) — Voice-over-IP radio linking standard utilizing dedicated RF nodes.
  • 💬 eHam.net Portal — Product reviews, active ham forums, community articles, and classified listings.
  • 📦 QSL.net Server Space — Free web hosting and email forwarding hubs dedicated strictly to licensed amateurs.
  • 🛒 QTH.com Swapmeet — Classified ad trading spaces and premium ham radio website hosting pipelines.

Federal & Regulatory Portals

  • 📡 FCC Amateur Radio Service Portal — Core licensing rules and service parameters.
  • 📜 FCC Part 97 Rules Reference Code — Federal regulatory guidelines governing operations.
  • 🗃️ FCC Universal Licensing System (ULS) — Official federal master license database and filing dashboard.

Surrounding Regional Clubs & Systems

  • 🗼 Azalea Coast Amateur Radio Club (AC4RC) — Operating out of the greater Wilmington, NC area.
  • 🔗 Carolina 440 UHF Link System — Interconnected wide-coverage repeater matrix serving NC and SC.
  • 🛰️ Carolina DX Association (CDXA) — Dedicated long-distance communication and contesting group.
  • 🌊 Wilmington VHF Association (N4ILM) — Managing wide-area communication links across southeast NC.
  • ⛰️ Shelby Amateur Radio Club — Organizers of the historic regional autumn hamfest.
  • 🏛️ Asheville Radio Museum — Historic preservation site showing the history of early voice gear.
  • 🎟️ Dayton Hamvention Main Site — The premier national amateur radio convention and swap exhibition.
