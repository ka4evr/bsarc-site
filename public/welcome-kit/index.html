Required Training Courses

To participate fully in official emergency service deployments, operators are highly encouraged to complete the following free FEMA independent study courses:

  • IS-100 (Introduction to the Incident Command System)
  • IS-200 (Basic Incident Command System for Initial Response)
  • IS-700 (National Incident Management System, An Introduction)
  • IS-800 (National Response Framework, An Introduction)

Note: The ICS-205 (Incident Radio Communications Plan) form is a critical asset utilized during active emergency operations to coordinate channel plans.

ARRL Membership Support

Are you planning on renewing your ARRL membership or joining for the first time? Please process your application directly through our Club Treasurer! When renewals or new memberships are handled locally through the club, BSARC receives a direct financial commission from the ARRL to help fund our local repeater installations and emergency gear. See our weekly newsletter or check in at the breakfast table for details!

FCC License Renewals

Is your ten-year amateur license approaching its expiration date? Don’t fall for third-party processing scams that charge extra middleman fees! You can easily handle your paperwork online or via mail yourself.

We’ve put together a step-by-step walkthrough covering the 90-day filing window, the 2-year grace period, and how to process the mandatory $35.00 FCC application fee. 👉 Check out our BSARC FCC License Renewal Guide for full instructions.
