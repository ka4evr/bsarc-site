# ARRL Membership Application & Renewal Program

Did you know you can support BSARC directly when joining or renewing your ARRL membership? As an official ARRL Affiliated Club, we are commissioned to process applications locally. 

When you route your enrollment through the club, the ARRL passes a portion of your standard dues back to our treasury to help fund local repeater maintenance and public service equipment—**at absolutely zero extra cost to you!**

### Club Commissions:
* 💰 **New ARRL Membership:** The club receives **$15.00**
* 🔄 **Annual ARRL Renewal:** The club receives **$5.00**

## Simple Step-by-Step Instructions

Follow these simple steps to ensure the club receives credit for your application or annual renewal:

1. **Download and Print the ARRL Application:** Click the link below to pull up the official document:  
   📄 [Official ARRL Membership Application Form (PDF)](https://www.arrl.org/files/file/Membership/Club%20Commission/ARRL%20Membership%20Application%20CLUB%20rev%20Jan%202024.pdf)  
   *\*Note: Print the form, check whether this is a "New" or "Renewal" account, and circle the **One-Year Standard Membership** option in the center column.*

2. **Download and Print the Club Reconciliation Form:** Open and print our local verification sheet:  
   📄 [BSARC Club Commission Reconciliation Form (PDF)](/pdf/ccr_form.pdf)  
   *\*Note: Fill out the center section indicating your status and the appropriate commission amount ($15 for New, $5 for Renewal).*

3. **Mail Both Documents to HQ:** Place both completed forms (along with your payment check if paying by mail) into a single envelope and send it directly to the ARRL headquarters address:

   > 📬 **ARRL Headquarters** > 225 Main Street  
   > Newington, CT 06111-1400

4. **Notify the Treasurer:** Drop a quick email to the <a href="mailto:treasurer@n4gm.org" style="color: #357edd; text-decoration: none; font-weight: bold;">Club Treasurer</a> letting them know you have mailed your forms off to Newington so they can verify the incoming commission credit when it hits our account balance.
