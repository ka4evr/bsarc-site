# Amateur Radio Web Directory

A comprehensive collection of essential amateur radio tools, federal databases, equipment modifications, and surrounding regional club portals. Local interest portals and emergency services links are highlighted below.

---

### Technical Tools & Calculators
* 🧮 [Amateur Radio RF Exposure Safety Calculator](http://hintlink.com/power_density.htm) — Quick power density analysis to verify station compliance.
* 🔧 [Modifications for Ham Rigs (Mods.dk)](https://www.mods.dk/) — Manuals, schematics, and alignment steps for commercial transceivers.
* 📻 [Nostalgia Air Antique Radio Resource](http://www.nostalgiaair.org/) — Vintage tube data files and schematic archives for historic gear restorers.
* ⏰ [NIST WWV Time Signal Station Portal](https://www.nist.gov/pml/time-and-frequency-division/radio-stations/wwv) — Official station operational guidelines and transmission details.

---

### National & State Training Organizations
* 🏛️ [ARRL Main Portal](https://www.arrl.org/) — National association for amateur radio in the US.
* 🗺️ [ARRL North Carolina Section](https://www.ncarrl.org/) — Local section news, event tracking, and field appointments.
* 🚨 [North Carolina ARES Hub](http://www.ncarrl.org/ares/) — State emergency network coordination, plans, and training matrices.
* 🌪️ [VoIP SKYWARN / Hurricane Net](http://voipwx.net/) — Real-time severe weather tactical reporting infrastructure.
* 💻 [FEMA Independent Study Directory](https://training.fema.gov/is/crslist.aspx) — Free online NIMS/ICS core training coursework.

---

### Operating Platforms & Networks
* 🗺️ [EchoLink Portal](https://www.echolink.org/) — Global internet-linking software engine for licensed operators.
* 🌐 [Internet Radio Linking Project (IRLP)](http://www.irlp.net/) — Voice-over-IP radio linking standard utilizing dedicated RF nodes.
* 💬 [eHam.net Portal](https://www.eham.net/) — Product reviews, active ham forums, community articles, and classified listings.
* 📦 [QSL.net Server Space](https://www.qsl.net/) — Free web hosting and email forwarding hubs dedicated strictly to licensed amateurs.
* 🛒 [QTH.com Swapmeet](https://www.qth.com/) — Classified ad trading spaces and premium ham radio website hosting pipelines.

---

### Federal & Regulatory Portals
* 📡 [FCC Amateur Radio Service Portal](https://www.fcc.gov/wireless/bureau-division/mobility-division/amateur-radio-service) — Core licensing rules and service parameters.
* 📜 [FCC Part 97 Rules Reference Code](https://www.arrl.org/part-97-amateur-radio-service) — Federal regulatory guidelines governing operations.
* 🗃️ [FCC Universal Licensing System (ULS)](https://www.fcc.gov/wireless/universal-licensing-system) — Official federal master license database and filing dashboard.
